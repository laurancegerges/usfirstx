Information For Sellers

When you place an order to sell foreign currency online, you'll receive detailed instructions for securing your order. Here are some tips to ensure your shipment is secure, traceable, and expedited. For more information, read our complete set of shipping instructions before you ship your parcel.

  • Keep track of your shipment

    Be sure to keep track of your shipment through FedEx's tracking portal. If you suspect your shipment has been lost, contact FedEx with your tracking number to check the status of your shipment.

  • Keep your shipment private

    Never show anything valuable to strangers, and don't tell shipping clerks that your package contains currency.

  • Email us your tracking number and quote number

    As soon as you ship your package, email us your tracking number and your quote number so we can monitor your shipment.
