US First Exchange offers three convenient ways to your receive funds.

When will I receive my payment?

US First Exchange takes the security of your package and your payment very seriously. When you create an order to sell foreign currency online, you'll receive shipping instructions via email and you'll be able to choose from three convenient options to receive payment for your currency. Be sure to follow all shipping instructions to expedite processing of your order.

Once your funds are received, your order will be validated by the US First Exchange team. Payments are normally issued within 24 hours of receipt and processing. You can monitor transit and reception through tracking information.

Three Convenient Ways to Get Paid

E-check

An electronic check is the quickest and cheapest method to receive your money (no fees apply). You'll receive an email from us with a printable check. Simply print and deposit as you would any regular check.

Wire Transfer

You can choose same day transfer or 2 day delivery. Fees assessed by our financial institution and your bank will apply.

Check by Mail

You can choose to have your check sent overnight or 2nd day delivery. Standard postal/shipping fees will apply.
